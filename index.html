Seduce your senses with gourmet Japanese cuisine at Ronin, the ultimate Japanese restaurant in Dubai. Catch the live cooking action at the teppanyaki counter, explore sushi making at the chef led sushi bar, or dine overlooking the hustle and bustle of the restaurant from our private pagoda style dining. Explore the tales of the ronin, unwritten stories of wanderlust immortalized in irezumi tattoo art decorating the restaurant, whilst Chef SK guides you through the culinary rebellion of the ronin.
